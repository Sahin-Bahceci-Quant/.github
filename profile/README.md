# Sahin-Bahceci Quant Research

Collaborative quantitative research and algorithmic trading experiments 
by two students from Turkey — one from Electrical & Electronics Engineering, 
one from Mathematics.

## What we do

We explore systematic trading strategies, market microstructure, 
and quantitative methods as we prepare for careers in quantitative 
finance. This organization hosts our research notebooks, backtesting 
frameworks, and ongoing projects.

## Current focus areas

- Cryptocurrency systematic strategies (Freqtrade-based)
- Forex algorithmic trading (Backtrader-based)  
- Market microstructure and execution analysis
- Statistical arbitrage and mean-reversion research
